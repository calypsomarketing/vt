ABOUT ME

A Journey From City Courtrooms to Wild Oceans

THE START People are in disbelief when they learn I haven't been freediving and spearfishing my whole life! Originally from Montréal, I'm a Canadian lawyer who worked in finance in London for 6 years and joined governments in several countries to draft a progressive legislation on fisheries. 

THEN I quit it all to hunt for my own food, stare my greatest fears in the eye and live a liberating life addicted to nature, adrenaline, exploration and responsible consumption.

TODAY I am a spearfisherwoman, thrill-seeker, accomplished chef, global traveller and advocate of sustainable eating. My life touches all corners of the Earth to discover cultures, fish with locals, learn their methods and cook and share the best food caught by myself in a way that respects nature.

A free-diving huntress on a sustainable mission

INDUSTRY &  CONSCIOUS  CONSUMPTION The exponential increase of a general curiosity in natural food, good sources and clean eating is pushing people to take informed decisions on what they consume. Spearfishing offers an interesting opportunity to educate, illuminate and truly understand the provenance of fish and seafood we’re accustomed to as well as the impact of humans on ocean-life as a whole.

THE GREAT DISCONNECT People are disconnected from nature and where food really comes from. As a result, mass industries exploit malpractices that are extremely detrimental to the environment. I plunge into the wild unknown anywhere on the planet, to connect people closer to nature, where their food comes from.

MISSION

MISSION Above all, it is my mission to demystify the global span of fishing cultures and educate global audiences about the incredible advantages of local and sustainable fishing and sourcing through inspiring, exciting and educational content that shares the adrenaline-filled adventures of a fearless modern woman.

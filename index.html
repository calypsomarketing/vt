About me

Freediving Instructor, Spearfisherwoman

I am a trained lawyer in Canada and worked in finance in London for 6 years. A freediving instructor with PADI, professional traveller, accomplished chef, advocate of sustainable eating, I quit my well-paid job in London to live a simple life and to spread the word about eating responsibly. I travel the world to fish and hunt with locals in order to eat the best food, caught by myself, in an eco-friendly way.
